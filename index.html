The Courses

Three rounds. Three distinct tests. The Mornington Peninsula at its finest — and most unforgiving.

Round 1
Friday Morning  ·  7:30am Tee Off

Moonah Links

Legends Course

The older sibling. Linksland that bites. Named after legends of the game — which sets expectations for everyone involved, most of whom are legends only in their own group chats. The Legends Course rewards ball-strikers and punishes the loose. Wind off the bay makes every club selection a negotiation. Brendan once topped the first tee here so comprehensively the ball finished behind him. That story has never died. It never will.

Moonah Links

The younger, meaner sibling — designed to host the Australian Open, which tells you everything about the gap between that field and this one. St Andrews Beach

Golf Club

The showpiece. The one everyone's been waiting for. St Andrews Beach is a genuine world-class links layout that demands respect and repays bottle. Sweeping views, rolling fairways, and enough character to make or break a scorecard on any given hole. This is where The Vase gets decided. Anyone still in contention by the turn has earned their shot. The group chat has referenced St Andrews in a previous trip — it already carries weight. Saturday morning, 9am. Let's go.

Off the Course

Watering Holes

The committee has shortlisted the options. A decision will be made in due course. All venues are capable of producing incidents that will be discussed for years.

Fireplace Confirmed

Rosebud Hotel

📍 Rosebud, Mornington Peninsula

The reliable. The known quantity. Fireplace that Brad will be stoking by 9pm. The embers outlast most of the group. Brendan operates in the corner. Buff laughs loud enough to turn heads across the room. A classic golf trip pub.

Option

Rye Hotel

📍 Rye, Mornington Peninsula

Local stalwart. The kind of pub that doesn't need to try hard because it doesn't need to. Cold beer, loud enough to talk over. Rye is close, which matters after 36 holes and nobody wanting to be in a car for long.

Option

Continental Hotel Sorrento

📍 Ocean Beach Rd, Sorrento VIC 3943

The Conti. A step up in ambience, which means a step up in the chance someone orders something off the wine list and gets roasted for it. Sorrento has energy. The group brings its own. Outcomes unpredictable.

Option

Sorrento Hotel

📍 Ocean Beach Rd, Sorrento VIC 3943

The alternative Sorrento option. Same town, different vibe. Decent beer garden, solid food, the sort of place that absorbs a large group without complaint. Toss-up between this and the Conti will be resolved on the night.

Wildcard

Local Footy

📍 Peninsula, wherever the bounce of the ball takes us

If there's footy on. If it's accessible. If the vibe is right. Nothing bonds a group like being around strangers who don't know any of the backstory. The Drain Man will find the bar within 90 seconds. The Gow will have a strong opinion about the umpiring.

How It Works

Format & Rules

The rules are simple. The handicaps are controversial. The trash talk is mandatory.

🏗

Stroke Play

Full handicap applied. Net score counts. If you're posting 110+ off a 23, the committee reserves the right to raise an eyebrow.

🅾
